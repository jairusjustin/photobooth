About Photobooth

Moments unfold. Photobooth makes them simple, timeless, and yours—capture a smile, a glance, a fleeting instant, and transform it into a memory you can revisit, share, and treasure

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the photobooth work?

Allow camera access, hit capture, and your photo is generated instantly.

Can I retake my photos?

Yes, retake as many times as you like before saving or sharing.

Do I need to install anything?

No, it works directly in your browser—no downloads required.

What types of photos can I capture?

Currently, you can create single polaroids or 3-shot strips. More options may be added in the future.

Is it mobile-friendly?

Yes, it works directly in your browser on any device with camera permissions enabled.
