About Photobooth

Our Photobooth web app brings the fun of instant photo strips to your browser. Capture memories, create polaroid-style strips, and share them effortlessly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the photobooth work?

Just allow camera access, click the capture button, and your photo strip will be generated instantly.

Can I retake my photos?

Yes, you can retake until you are satisfied before saving or sharing.

Do I need to install anything?

No installation needed—our photobooth works right from your browser.

Can I use the photobooth on mobile devices?

Yes, the photobooth is mobile-friendly, but make sure your browser allows camera access.

Why is the screen black even after allowing camera access?

This usually happens if your browser hasn’t refreshed the camera feed. Try reloading the page or checking camera permissions.

Why can’t I take a photo after starting and selecting a style?

The photobooth requires camera access before you can take photos. The modal will stay on screen until you grant camera permission. Once allowed, the modal will close automatically and you can proceed with capturing your photo.

Does the photobooth store my photos or personal data?

No, your photos are only stored temporarily in your browser. We do not collect or store any personal data.
