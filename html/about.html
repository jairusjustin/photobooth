About

A modern way to capture moments—simple, timeless, and always within reach. Our Photobooth web app lets you create polaroid-style strips that feel personal and lasting. Clean, effortless, and made for memories worth keeping.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the photobooth work?

Allow camera access, hit capture, and your photo strip is generated instantly.

Can I retake my photos?

Yes, retake as many times as you like before saving or sharing.

Do I need to install anything?

No, it works directly in your browser—no downloads required.

Is it mobile-friendly?

Yes, just ensure your browser allows camera access.

Why is my screen black?

Refresh the page or check your camera permissions.

Why can’t I take a photo?

Camera access is required. The modal closes once permission is granted.

Does it store my photos?

No, photos stay only in your browser—we don’t collect data.
