Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Our Photobooth does not collect, share, or store personal data.

Photos are kept only in your browser and never sent to a server.

Camera access is managed by your browser, and you can revoke it at any time. For security, we recommend using trusted devices and browsers.

By using the Photobooth, you agree to this policy.
