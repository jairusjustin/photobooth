Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Our Photobooth web app does not collect or store any personal data from users.

All photos captured are temporarily stored in your browser and are not sent to any server.

We recommend using the app on secure devices and browsers. Camera permissions are handled directly by your browser, and you can revoke access at any time.

By using our photobooth, you acknowledge and agree to this privacy policy.
